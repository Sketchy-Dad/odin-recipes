Biscuits and Gravy!

Enjoy this classic Southern breakfast dish, perfect for a hearty start to your day!

This biscuits and gravy recipe is a comforting breakfast favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 lb breakfast sausage
  • 2 cups milk (for gravy)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (for gravy)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the milk until just combined, then turn onto a floured surface and knead gently.
  5. Roll out to 1-inch thickness and cut into rounds.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 12 -15 minutes until golden.
  7. Meanwhile, cook the sausage in a skillet until browned.
  8. In a separate pot, whisk together 2 cups milk and 1/4 cup flour over medium heat until thickened.
  9. Season with salt and pepper, then stir in the cooked sausage.
  10. Serve the biscuits topped with the sausage gravy.
