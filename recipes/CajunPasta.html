Cajun Pasta

Experience the bold flavors of this Cajun pasta recipe, perfect for a quick dinner!

This Cajun pasta is a delightful blend of spices and creamy sauce!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, saut√© the onion and bell pepper until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and shrimp, cooking until shrimp are pink.
  4. Stir in the Cajun seasoning and heavy cream, simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce, season with salt and pepper.
