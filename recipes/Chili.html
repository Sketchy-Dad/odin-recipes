Homemade Chili

Enjoy this hearty and flavorful chili recipe, perfect for any occasion!

This chili recipe is perfect for a cozy night in!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the kidney beans and diced tomatoes, simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
